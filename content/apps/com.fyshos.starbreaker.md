Starbreaker is a neon vector take on the arcade classic Asteroids, built with the Fyne toolkit.
Drift through a glowing nebula, blast the rocks into ever-smaller pieces, dodge the hunting saucer and clear wave after escalating wave.
It is a showcase for two new Fyne canvas primitives: arbitrary stroked polygons for the ship and rocks, and an animated GLSL shader backdrop.
