Explore the solar system from your desktop.
Orbitly renders the sun and the planets in orbit, with a starfield behind and glowing orbital paths, animating our local planets.

Run the guided tour or click a planet to focus on it. Read interesting facts and explore the layers that make up the visible planet.
Play, pause or speed up time to watch the planets move, or follow the guided tour to visit each body in turn.
