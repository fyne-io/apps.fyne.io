&#34;Karma Manager&#34; is an anagram of &#34;Anagram Maker&#34;.  Anagrams are words or phrase the use the same letters as each other.  Ever wonder what is hidden in your name?  Your school&#39;s name? Your favorite saying?  The phrase &#34;A pet dragon&#34; anagrams to &#34;Entrap a god&#34;.  How about &#34;Cal State Chico ↔️ coastal, hectic&#34; (It&#39;s neither of those things, but that&#39;s part of the fun.)  Once you&#39;re found one you like, you can save it to a collection of Favorites, animate them, and share them with friends!
